# EV Market Sizing Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Estimate the market size and growth potential of the Electric Vehicle (EV) industry.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel
- Market Research Analysis
- Assumption-Based Modeling

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed
- Market segmentation analysis
- Total Addressable Market (TAM) estimation
- Growth rate projection
- Competitive landscape overview

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Rapid EV adoption driven by sustainability & policy support
- Strong growth potential in urban markets
- Government incentives significantly impact penetration rates
